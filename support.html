Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I restore my purchases?
A: Go to the Black Market tab in the app and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the screen.

Q: My stats aren't syncing across devices.
A: Ensure you are logged in with the exact same Apple ID on both devices and that iCloud Drive is enabled for SHADE in your iOS settings.
