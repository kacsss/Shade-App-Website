I built SHADE because I found myself frustrated with social deduction games that always played out exactly the same way.

Most games give you a role and expect you to do some basic lying, but I wanted something that actually forced you to think differently. The mechanics of the Double Agent and the Jester change the entire meta of how players interrogate each other.

SHADE is a game that learns from the chaos of its players. Trust no one. Establish your dossier.
