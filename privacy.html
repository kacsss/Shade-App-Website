Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

1. Data Collection

SHADE uses Sign in with Apple to establish your Persistent Dossier. We collect your Apple ID (unique identifier) and your game statistics (win rates, tier ranking, and squad history) to ensure your career progress follows you across devices.

2. Cloud Synchronization

Your game data is synced via secure cloud services to maintain your custom groups and leaderboards. We do not track your location, access your contacts, or read your messages.

3. Data Storage & Infrastructure

To provide cloud-synced squads and leaderboards, your game data is securely transmitted and stored using trusted third-party infrastructure. We utilize Hetzner for our server hosting and MongoDB for database management. Your data travels encrypted through these services solely for the purpose of operating the game and maintaining your Dossier.

4. Third-Party Services & Authentication

We rely on Apple’s native systems (Sign in with Apple) for secure authentication. We are firmly committed to your privacy: we do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers, marketers, or data brokers.
