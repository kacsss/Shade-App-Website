Privacy Protocol

Effective Date: April 2026

1. Data Collection

SHADE uses Sign in with Apple to establish your Persistent Dossier. We collect your Apple ID (unique identifier) and your game statistics (win rates, tier ranking, and squad history).

2. Cloud Synchronization

Your Dossier is synced via secure cloud services to ensure your career progress follows you across devices. We do not track your location or access your contacts.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Apple’s native systems for authentication and Game Center integration. No data is sold to third-party advertisers.
